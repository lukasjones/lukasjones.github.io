What is pair programming

  • Pair programming is a way for students to collaborate on their work.
  • allows for a better technical understanding of the material.
  • It offers you the perspective of your partner and their apporach to solving problems
  • It serves to motivate students and provide accountability checks.

Why is pair programming important to DBC?

  • prepares us for the working environment at DBC.
  • to ensure that we're more confident with the material before they go onsite.
  • allows DBC to track our progress.
  • Prepares us with collaboration skills that we will use in the work force.
